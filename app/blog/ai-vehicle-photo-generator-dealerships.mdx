## Why Lot Photos Lose Sales

The problem with lot photography isn't the photographer; it's the lighting, the background, and the speed.

**Lot photo reality:**
- Taken in a parking lot or row of cars (distracting background)
- Often shot in harsh sunlight or flat overcast light (unflattering shadows)
- Car is surrounded by other inventory (cheap appearance)
- Shot in 5 minutes with an iPhone (inconsistent quality)
- Published immediately (no time for editing)

**Result:** The car looks cheap, suspicious, or overpriced. Buyers scroll past.

Meanwhile, a professional studio photo (shot in a controlled environment with premium lighting) makes the same car look expensive, desirable, and trustworthy. The difference in appearance is so stark that the same car in a lot photo vs. a studio photo might get 5x the clicks.

The problem is that studio photography is expensive and slow—you can't do it for every vehicle every week.

## How AI Vehicle Photo Generation Works (In Plain English)

The technology is called "diffusion-based image generation." Here's what happens:

1. **You provide a reference photo** — A snapshot of the vehicle from your lot (any lighting, any angle, low resolution is fine)

2. **AI rebuilds the car** — The model analyzes the reference image and reconstructs the car:
   - What color is it?
   - What's the body shape?
   - What are the wheels, trim, badges?
   - What's the condition?

3. **AI places the car in a premium environment** — You select a backdrop (urban luxury showroom, coastal cliffside, modern gallery, etc.) and the AI renders the car in that environment with:
   - Professional lighting (ambient + accent lights)
   - Realistic shadows and reflections
   - Premium background (no other cars, no parking lot)
   - Cinematic composition

4. **Output is a photoreal image** — The result looks like a professional studio photo, dimensioned for your channel (Instagram 1080×1350, website 600×600, print 8.5"×11", etc.)

**Time: 5 minutes. Cost: $0 (platform fee covers unlimited generations).**

## Quality Considerations

AI photos are excellent but not flawless. Common artifacts:

- **Occasional odd reflections** — A reflection that doesn't quite match real-world physics (very rare)
- **Wheel generation** — Wheels sometimes look slightly stylized (minor issue)
- **Interior photos** — Less reliable than exterior (interior lighting is harder for AI to predict)
- **Extreme angles** — Very low or very high angles sometimes look unnatural (avoid; use normal angles)

**Workaround:** Generate in batches, skip obvious duds, use the best 5 of 8. Takes 5 minutes to curate.
